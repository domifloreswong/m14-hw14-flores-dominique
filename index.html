AND SO SHE DID!
Empowered women empower women - this is something I am incredibly in accordance with. I wish to pay forward all of the amazing inspiration I have received in my journey, from powerful women, by hopefully being able to help others myself.

Women@Disney

I have the incredible honor of leading the Women@Disney Business Employee Resource Group - a grassroots, employee-led effort, with the vision of creating an inclusive community valuing the unique impact of every woman's voice. We want to improve culture and enhance professional development.

Content Curator

As a Content Curator for Training & Development at Disney Vacation Club (a subsidiary of Disney Signature Experiences, a division of Disney Parks, Experiences, and Products, a segment of The Walt Disney Company), I create Magic backstage while honing my storytelling skills, and creating engaging content. I lead analysis of our client's learning needs, strategically developing, designing, managing, and communicating Learning and Development solutions. Creatively designing theories to achieve learning goals, develop evaluation strategies and metrics to be able to track impact. I thoroughly enjoy being a collaborative business consultant, and cross-functional leadership partner - with internal and external stakeholders throughout the enterprise.

DE&I Champion

As a Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion champion, I lend my passion to volunteer and stretch projects across The Walt Disney Company by co-leading the Women at Disney BERG (business employee resource group) Talent Mobility, Learning & Development pillar. I also lead and collaborate DE&I strategy and communications across Disney Signature Experiences, recently rolling out an intern and rotational program targetting HBCUs, with the goal of increasing diversity in our workforce. I aim to continue developing and fostering relationships, collaborating in the continuous creation and communication of engaging content, whether it be for learning and development spaces, DE&I spaces, or mass communications.

Voluneerism

I enjoy lending my time volunteering with STEM and children organizations like FIRST Robotics and VEX Robotics, as a mentor, coach, and tournament director in Central Florida. I've had the opportunity to creatively manage social media accounts and create content for non-profits, freelance artists, businesses, influencers, and in DE&I spaces.
